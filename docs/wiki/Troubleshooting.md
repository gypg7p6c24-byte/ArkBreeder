# Troubleshooting

## Imported creatures look incoherent
- Import `GameUserSettings.ini` and `Game.ini` again.
- Check manual overrides in **Settings**.
- Re-export the creature and re-import.

## No breeding plan shown
- Confirm both male and female exist for the species.
- Verify species values are loaded.

## Wrong level budget
- Review `Max wild level cap`, `OverrideOfficialDifficulty`, and `DifficultyOffset`.
